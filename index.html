About Me

I am a Research Associate at Technische Universität Dresden, specializing in traffic process automation. My research focuses on leveraging machine learning and AI-driven methods to enhance traffic systems and optimize urban mobility networks.

I hold an MSc degree in Computational Modelling and Simulation from Technische Universität Dresden.
