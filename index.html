About Me

I am a Research Associate at Technische Universität Dresden in the "Friedrich List" Faculty of Transport and Traffic Sciences. My work focuses on advanced data-driven methods for traffic optimization, machine learning for big traffic data analysis, and AI-centric mobility systems.

Research Interests:

  • Machine Learning for Traffic Big Data Analysis
  • Data-driven Approach for Traffic Optimization
  • AI-centric Mobility Systems
  • Advanced Driving Assistant Systems (ADAS)

I hold an MSc degree in Computational Modelling and Simulation from Technische Universität Dresden.
