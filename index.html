Jyotirmaya Ijaradar

I am a Research Associate at Technische Universität Dresden within the "Friedrich List" Faculty of Transport and Traffic Sciences, specifically at the Chair of Traffic Process Automation.

Research: My primary research areas include:

  • Machine Learning for Traffic Big Data Analysis
  • Data-driven Approach for Traffic Optimization
  • Development of AI-centric methods for autonomous mobility systems
  • Advanced Driving Assistant System (ADAS)

I focus on leveraging AI and data-driven methodologies to enhance autonomous mobility systems and traffic optimization.

Bio: I hold an MSc degree in Computational Modelling and Simulation from Technische Universität Dresden. I also actively contribute to cultural activities through Bongo Utsav Dresden (BUD) e.V..
